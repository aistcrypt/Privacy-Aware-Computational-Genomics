Call for Talks/Posters

Abstracts for considerations should be 300 – 600 words and submitted from following system:
the Easy Chair site for PRIVAGEN 2015.

The submitted abstracts are reviewed by the program committee and a few of them will be selected as oral presentations. All the abstracts will be considered for oral presentations unless the author specify their submission is for poster presentation only.

The notification whether each abstract is presented as a talk or a poster will be announced by XX.

PRIVAGEN accepts both unpublished and published works, and the submissions of unpublished works are not considered as publication.

Topics Of Interest

Topics of interest include, but not limited to:

  • Privacy-preserving data-mining for genomics
  • Cryptographic techniques that potentially assist genomic sequence retrieval/medical information analyses/epi-genomic information analyses
  • Genome ethics
  • Privacy evaluation of personal genome
  • Genome-hacking
