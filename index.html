Welcome to Privacy-Aware-Computational-Genomics 2015

PRIVAGEN 2015 is an official satellite workshop of GIW/InCoB 2015 focusing on privacy-aware technologies that assists computational genomics, and aims to facilitate discussion among researchers in diverse fields including bioinformatics, genome ethics, data-mining and cryptography.

PRIVAGEN seeks abstract submissions to be peer-reviewed and selected either oral presentations or posters. It also hosts two keynotes by leaders in related fields, two invited talks and a panel discussion.
